Donate

Thank you for visiting Woodcock Nature Preserve’s website!

If you enjoyed our preserve and want to help out, we would be more than happy to accept donations. Donations help to fund our ability to maintain and improve any pre-existing buildings on the property (two houses and the workshop barn), trails, porta-potties, and our property taxes. We work with donations and volunteers to improve wildlife habitats, bird research, and to maintain this website. In addition to the maintenance of our preserve, donations give us the ability to build more amenities, including but not limited to: benches, picnic tables, and a pond aerator! Any and all donations are greatly appreciated, and we hope you enjoy your time at WNP!

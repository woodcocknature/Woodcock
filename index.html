Our Mission

Fostering recreation, education and research managed grassland, woodland and food forest, Woodcock seeks to demonstrate how collaborative, sustainable land stewardship can be self-funded.

Collaborate

Woodcock invites the reader, and any outside people and groups of good will, to collaborate with us, using the lands and facilities at the preserve as an asset. Collaboration with Woodcock can take many forms. For some examples of collaboration happening between Woodcock Nature Preserve and outside groups, check out our Collaborate page!
